Read the text.

Your job is to do only 3 things:
1. extract keywords
2. extract relationships between the keywords
3. draw a tree-style mind map

Keyword rules:
- keywords can be nouns, verbs, adjectives, or short phrases
- keep important action words
- keep only words that carry meaning
- do not include filler words
- do not invent words

Relationship rules:
- use relationships that are clearly stated in the text
- use source wording where possible
- do not add extra meaning

Mind map rules:
- main topic at top
- related keywords grouped under it
- short nodes only
- no explanation outside the map

Output exactly in this form:

Keywords
[list of keywords]

Relationships
- keyword → relationship → keyword

Mind map
Main Topic
├── keyword / relationship
│   └── keyword
└── keyword / relationship
    └── keyword
